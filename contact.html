Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

Is the consultation really free? +
Yes, absolutely! Our initial consultation is completely free with no hidden charges. We believe in transparency and want to understand your needs before proposing any solutions.
How long does the admission process take? +
The timeline varies depending on the course and college. Typically, the process takes 2-4 weeks from document submission to final admission confirmation. We work efficiently to ensure quick processing.
Do you help with accommodation and other logistics? +
Yes, we provide end-to-end support including hostel arrangements, travel guidance, and orientation support. Our assistance continues even after admission to ensure a smooth transition.
What documents are required for admission? +
Common documents include: mark sheets (10th, 12th, graduation), entrance exam scorecard, ID proof (Aadhar/Passport), photographs, and caste certificate (if applicable). We'll provide a detailed checklist based on your specific course.
Can you guarantee admission to my preferred college? +
While we have strong partnerships with 200+ institutions and a 95% success rate, admissions depend on eligibility, seat availability, and college requirements. We work diligently to secure admission in the best possible institution matching your profile.
Do you provide education loans assistance? +
Yes, we guide students through the education loan process and have tie-ups with multiple banks and financial institutions. We assist with documentation and application procedures for education loans.
